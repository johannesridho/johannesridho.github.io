FOSSASIA Summit 2020

In March 2020, I gave a talk with title “High Performance Location-based Restaurant Campaigns in Foodpanda” in FOSSASIA Summit. This talk highlights how my team solve a problem where our service needs to return the list of campaigns based on the user location and the delivery areas of the restaurants participating in the campaigns. Our service can handle more than 400 thousand requests per minute, around 17 times more than the real peak throughput at that time, which is 23.7 thousand requests per minute.
